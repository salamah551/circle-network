## Color & Styling Guide

### Colors:
- **Background:** Black (#000000)
- **Primary Accent:** Amber (#F59E0B - amber-500, #D97706 - amber-600)
- **Text Primary:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Text Secondary:** Zinc-400 (#A1A1AA)
- **Text Tertiary:** Zinc-500 (#71717A)
- **Border Default:** Zinc-800 (#27272A)
- **Border Active:** Amber-500 (#F59E0B)
- **Success:** Emerald-400 (#34D399)
- **Error:** Red-400 (#F87171)

### Typography:
- **Hero Headlines:** 4xl-6xl, font-bold (48-72px)
- **Section Headlines:** 2xl-3xl, font-bold (24-36px)
- **Body Text:** base-xl (16-20px)
- **Small Text:** sm (14px)
- **Tiny Text:** xs (12px)

### Interactive Elements:
- **Primary Button:** 
  - Background: gradient amber-500 to amber-600
  - Text: black
  - Hover: gradient amber-600 to amber-700
  - Focus: ring amber-500

- **Secondary Button:**
  - Background: zinc-900
  - Border: zinc-800
  - Text: white
  - Hover: zinc-800

- **Selected Card:**
  - Background: amber-500/20 (amber with 20% opacity)
  - Border: amber-500 (2px)
  - Text: white

- **Unselected Card:**
  - Background: zinc-950
  - Border: zinc-800 (2px)
  - Text: zinc-400
  - Hover: border zinc-700

### Spacing:
- Container max-width: 3xl (768px)
- Vertical sections: 12 (48px)
- Card padding: 8 (32px)
- Button padding: px-6 py-3 (24px/12px)
- Gap between elements: 3-4 (12-16px)

### Animations:
- Transitions: 300ms duration
- Progress bar: 500ms smooth transition
- Hover effects: scale and color transitions
- Loading spinner: rotate animation

### Responsive Design:
- Mobile-first approach
- Breakpoints: sm (640px), md (768px), lg (1024px)
- Touch-friendly targets (minimum 44px)
- Stack on mobile, grid on desktop for cards
